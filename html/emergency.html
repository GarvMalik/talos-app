Emergency Protocols

If you or someone else is in immediate life-threatening danger, do not wait. Contact emergency services right away.

Medical Helpline (Päivystysapu)

Call 116 117 for urgent health problems when your local health center is closed.

MIELI Crisis Helpline

09 2525 0113. Available 24/7 for urgent mental health support in English.

Poison Information Centre

0800 147 111. Open 24/7 for advice on suspected poisonings.
